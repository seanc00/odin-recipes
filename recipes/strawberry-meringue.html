Strawberry Meringue

The sweetest treat which compliments every dessert!

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 225g caster sugar
  • 0.5 tsp vanilla and strawberry extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 140 degrees celsius, then separate yolk and egg whites.
  2. In a big bowl, whisk egg whites and add sugar periodically until stiff peaks.
  3. Once stiff peaks, add in the vanilla extract and whisk.
  4. Line a baking tray with baking paper and place well spaced out dollops of meringue.
  5. Put in oven for 1hr45min, then once done place meringue on cold rack to cool.
