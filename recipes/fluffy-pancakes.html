Fluffy Pancakes

The only food that will make you believe you're biting into a cloud!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 Tbsp milk
  • o.25 Tsp vanilla extract
  • 0.25 cup flour
  • 0.5 Tsp baking powder
  • 2.5 Tbsp sugar

Steps

  1. Separate egg yolk and egg whites.
  2. Whisk egg whites until stiff peaks.
  3. Add all other ingredients excluding egg whites, into a bowl and mix.
  4. Add egg whites to rest of ingredients and fold slowly.
  5. On the lowest heat, heat up a pan and add dollops of pancake mix.
  6. Place a few drops of water into the pan and place lid on top of pan.
  7. Once jiggly, flip it and repeat steps 5-7.
