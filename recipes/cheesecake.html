Cheesecake (Kinder Bueno)

The number one cheesecake of all time! (Unofficially confirmed by me!)

Ingredients

  • 400g cream cheese
  • 300ml double cream
  • 400g standard digestives
  • 50g unsalted butter
  • 5 Tbsp sweetener
  • 200g kinder bueno chocolate

Steps

  1. Place cream cheese and sweetener into one big bowl and mix.
  2. In another bowl, add double cream and whisk till stiff peaks.
  3. Add chocolate into another bowl and put in the microwave for 3 minutes on max heat.
  4. Repeat 'step 3' with butter, but microwave for only 30 seconds.
  5. Mix all ingredients into one bowl except for digestives and butter.
  6. Crush digestives into a fine powder and mix with butter in separate bowl.
  7. Grab a cheesecake tin, place digestive mix on bottom and flatten.
  8. Once all flattened, add cheesecake mix on top then chill in fridge.
